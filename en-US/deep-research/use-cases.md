## Use case overview

| Scenario | Example prompt |
|:---|:---|
| Industry / market research | Research the competitive landscape and key players in the domestic humanoid robotics sector in 2025 |
| Competitive analysis | Compare and analyze the product strategy differences between Notion and Obsidian |
| Investment due diligence | Compile the funding rounds and valuation changes of major LLM companies over the past two years |
| Academic literature review | Trace the development of RAG technology and its current main optimization directions |
| Policy analysis | Analyze the impact of the latest data security regulations on companies expanding overseas |
| Personal decision-making | I want to buy a laptop for video editing — help me research the leading options |
