OpenClaw is an AI assistant with a unique personality and long-term memory. In the Kimi desktop app, you can deploy a local OpenClaw instance with a single click and start chatting with it right away.

<Callout type="info">
If you don't have a local OpenClaw yet, go to the Kimi Claw page in the Kimi desktop app to create your own.
</Callout>

One-click deployment handles all of the following for you:

- **Automatic deployment** — Kimi deploys OpenClaw on your machine automatically. If you already have another OpenClaw installed locally, Kimi can clone its persona, memory, and skills with your permission — making migration seamless.
- **Automatic model configuration** — Kimi pre-configures the Kimi model, links your Kimi Code membership credits, and sets up Kimi Web Search for internet access. No API setup required.
- **Membership requirement** — One-click deployment requires an Allegretto plan or above.
- **Instance limit** — Each account supports a maximum of 1 Kimi Claw Desktop at a time. If you switch machines and deploy on a new one, you will be prompted to unlink the previous instance first.

## Step 1: Download the Kimi desktop app and upgrade your plan

First, download the Kimi desktop app and sign in to your account.

**System requirements**

| Item | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| Operating system | macOS or Windows |
| Membership tier | Allegretto or above |

After downloading, sign in to your Kimi account and confirm your membership is at the Allegretto level or above. If not, upgrade your plan before proceeding.

<Frames
  src="./images/desktop claw/upgrade plan.png"
  alt="Kimi desktop app — signed out"
/>

## Step 2: Deploy locally

Go to the Kimi Claw page in the Kimi desktop app, click **Deploy on my computer**, and follow the on-screen steps to set up your Kimi Claw Desktop.

<Frames
  src="./images/desktop claw/add bot.png"
  alt="Kimi Claw deployment page"
/>

<Callout type="warning">
Each account supports a maximum of 1 Kimi Claw Desktop. If you previously deployed on another machine, you will be asked to confirm whether to disconnect that instance before continuing.
</Callout>

**Migrate from an existing local OpenClaw**

If you have another version of OpenClaw installed on the same machine, the system will ask whether you want to copy the following data:
- Persona
- Memory
- Chat history
- Workspace files
You can choose which data to migrate based on your needs.

## Step 3: Set a nickname

Once deployment is complete, give your Kimi Claw Desktop a nickname of your choice.

<Frames
  src="./images/desktop claw/nickname.png"
  alt="Kimi Claw Desktop settings page"
/>

Once you've set a nickname, your Kimi Claw Desktop is ready to go — start chatting with it anytime.

<Callout type="tip">
After deployment, you can further customize your Kimi Claw Desktop by configuring its persona, installing skills, and setting up scheduled tasks to build a more personalized AI assistant.
</Callout>
