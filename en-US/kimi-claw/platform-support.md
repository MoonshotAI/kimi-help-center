## Supported platforms
Kimi Claw is available on the following platforms:

- **Web** — Full-featured access at [kimi.com](https://kimi.com), including conversations, settings, terminal, and workspace management.
- **Mobile** — Available on iOS and Android via the Kimi app.
- **Telegram** — Deploy Kimi Claw as a Telegram bot for on-the-go conversations. See the [Telegram Bot setup guide](/kimi-claw/telegram-bot) for details.

More platform integrations are on the roadmap. Check back for updates or follow Kimi's official channels for announcements.
