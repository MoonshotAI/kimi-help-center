Presets are Kimi's quick-prompt feature, letting you save frequently used prompts and invoke them with a single click in any session — no more repetitive typing.

## When should you use presets?

- **Recurring formatted tasks**: E.g., you always need Kimi to output meeting notes, weekly reports, or emails in a specific template
- **Reusable high-quality prompts**: Save well-tuned prompts for consistent, repeatable results
- **Common instruction shortcuts**: Everyday commands like "Summarize this article," "Translate to English," or "Organize in a table"

## How to add a preset？

1. Open [kimi.com](https://www.kimi.com/) or the Kimi app
2. Click the **"+"** button to the left of the chat input box, or locate the **Presets** entry point
3. Click **"Add Preset"**
4. Enter a title (for easy identification) and the content (the full prompt text)
5. Save

## How to use a preset

In any session's input box, click the Presets entry point and select a saved preset. The content will auto-fill into the input box. You can send it as-is, or edit it further before sending.

## Managing presets

- **Edit**: Click an existing preset to modify it
- **Delete**: Remove presets you no longer need
- **Reorder**: Move your most-used presets to the top for quick access

## Tips

- Save thoroughly tested, high-quality prompts as Presets to ensure consistent, reliable output every time
- Prepare a few Presets for different work scenarios (writing, analysis, translation, etc.) to quickly switch modes
- Presets support long text — even complex multi-step instructions can be saved
