Kimi supports self-service invoice generation for your membership payments.

## How to get an invoice？

Invoices are automatically sent to the email address registered with your payment provider (Airwallex, Stripe, Apple, or Google).

## Need a revised invoice or additional details?
- **Email:** membership@moonshot.ai with the following information:
- Original invoice
- Billing details (company name, address, state/province, email, VAT/Tax ID)

Singapore users: please also provide your GST registration number
