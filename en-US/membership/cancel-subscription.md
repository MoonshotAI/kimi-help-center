You can cancel auto-renewal at any time. After cancellation, your membership benefits remain active until the end of the current billing cycle.

## How to cancel auto-renewal?

Choose the method that matches how you originally subscribed:

### Via the Kimi website or app

1. Sign in to Kimi (web or mobile).
2. Go to **Settings → Subscription**.
3. Click **Cancel Auto-Renewal** or **Manage Subscription**.
4. Confirm the cancellation.

### Via Apple ID (iOS / Mac)

1. On your iPhone, open **Settings** and tap your name at the top.
2. Tap **Subscriptions**.
3. Find **Kimi** and tap **Cancel Subscription**.

On Mac: Open the App Store → click your profile → **Account Settings** → find Kimi under **Subscriptions** → **Manage** → **Cancel Subscription**.

### Via Google Play (Android)

1. Open the **Google Play Store** app.
2. Tap your profile icon → **Payments & subscriptions** → **Subscriptions**.
3. Find **Kimi** and tap **Cancel subscription**.
4. Follow the on-screen prompts to confirm.

> After cancelling, your benefits stay active through the end of the paid period. You can re-subscribe anytime from the Kimi Subscription page.
