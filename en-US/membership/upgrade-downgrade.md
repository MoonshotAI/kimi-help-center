You can adjust your Kimi membership at any time. Here's how upgrades and downgrades work.

## How to calculate upgrade price?

Upgrades will be prorated and any unused portion of your current plan will be refunded.

| Item | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Effective** | Upgrade takes effect immediately, new credit allotment available right away |
| **Credits** | Credits already used under the old plan don't affect the new plan's balance — you receive a fresh allocation |

## Want to upgrade/downgrade?

### Upgrading

1. Visit [Kimi.com](https://www.kimi.com/) and click your avatar (bottom left) 
2. Choose your preferred plan and complete the payment
3. The system calculates the prorated difference, processes payment, and activates the new plan instantly

### Downgrading

<Callout type="warning">
Downgrades can't be applied mid-cycle.
</Callout>

1. Cancel your current subscription (see [Cancel Subscription](/help/membership/membership-cancel-subscription))
2. Continue using your current plan until it expires
3. Once expired, subscribe to the lower-tier plan of your choice

## Can't upgrade?

If the upgrade option isn't available or the process fails:

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| **Subscribed via Apple App Store or Google Play** | Manage your subscription through that platform's settings |
| **Payment method issue** | Verify your saved payment method is valid and has sufficient funds |
| **System error** | Try signing out and back in |

<Callout type="info">
If the problem persists, contact **membership@moonshot.ai** for assistance.
</Callout>
