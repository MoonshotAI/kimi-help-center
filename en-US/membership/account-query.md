# Account & credit query

<Callout type="info">
After purchasing a membership, your benefits typically activate instantly. If something seems off, check the guides below.
</Callout>

## Credits not showing after purchase?
If payment went through but your membership hasn't activated:
1. **Verify the charge** — Check your bank or payment provider to confirm the transaction completed. Some methods have a short delay; wait a few minutes, then refresh the page
2. **Check your account** — Make sure you're signed into the same Kimi account you used during checkout. If you have multiple accounts, try switching
3. **Sign out and back in** — A fresh login often refreshes your membership status

<Callout type="info">
If the issue persists, email **membership@moonshot.ai** with a screenshot of the charge and your account details.
</Callout>

## Where to check remaining credits?
### Web (kimi.com)
1. Click your avatar or profile icon in the bottom-left corner
2. Go to **Settings → Subscription**
3. Your current plan and remaining credits are displayed here
### Mobile (iOS / Android)
1. Open the Kimi app and tap the **Profile** tab
2. Go to **Membership**
3. You'll see your plan details and credit usage breakdown
