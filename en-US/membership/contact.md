Need help? Here's how to reach us.

## Billing & membership support

**Email:** membership@moonshot.ai

Best for:

- Credits not appearing after purchase
- Payment or charge issues
- Plan upgrades, downgrades, or cancellations
- Invoice requests

Paid members receive priority response. Please include your account email and any relevant screenshots to help us resolve your issue faster.

## General support

**Email:** support@moonshot.ai

Best for:

- Product feature questions
- Technical issues while using Kimi
- Account-related questions (non-billing)
- Feature requests and feedback

## In-product feedback

You can also submit feedback directly inside Kimi:

- **Web:** Click the **Feedback** button in the bottom-left corner, describe your issue, and submit.
- **Mobile:** Go to **Profile → Help & Feedback → Submit Feedback**.

In-product reports automatically attach your device and account context, which helps us diagnose issues faster.
