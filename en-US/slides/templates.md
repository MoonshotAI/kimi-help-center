# More Slides templates

<Callout type="info">
Open [Kimi Slides](https://www.kimi.com/slides) and enter your requirements. After generating an outline, you'll see two mode options below it. Click either mode to browse additional templates.
</Callout>

## Mode options

| Mode | Description | Time |
|------|-------------|------|
| **Adaptive** | Kimi intelligently designs layout and styling. Deep research + structured delivery | 30–60 min |
| **Visual** | Select from pre-made templates. Powered by Nano Banana Pro — ideal for quick presentations | 5–10 min |
