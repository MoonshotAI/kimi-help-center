# Account suspension & appeals

<Callout type="warning">
If you violate applicable laws, the user agreement, or platform policies, the platform may take actions including blocking content, freezing/transferring accounts, suspending benefits, or terminating service.
</Callout>

## Why was my account suspended?
Possible actions include:
| Action | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Content** | Block related content |
| **Account** | Freeze, close, or transfer the account |
| **Benefits** | Suspend or revoke some or all benefits (including paid benefits) |
| **Service** | Refuse, suspend, or terminate service |

## How to appeal？
If you disagree with the outcome:

<Callout type="info">
Email [support@moonshot.ai](mailto:support@moonshot.ai) to submit an appeal. We will investigate and respond as soon as possible.
</Callout>
