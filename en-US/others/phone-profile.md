## Can I change my linked email or sign-in method?
Yes. Navigate to:
**Kimi App** → **Profile** → **Settings** → **Account Security** → **Email** → **Change**
Follow the prompts to verify your current email, then enter and verify the new one.
## Lost access to your original sign-in method?
Please email [support@moonshot.ai](mailto:support@moonshot.ai). Our team will assist with the account transfer after verifying the required proof of ownership.
## Not receiving a verification code?
Try the following troubleshooting steps:
1. Confirm that the email address or phone number you entered is correct.
2. The verification code may take a few minutes to arrive — please be patient.
3. If it hasn't arrived after 5 minutes, click "Resend" to request a new code.

<Callout type="warning">
**Note**: Currently, only supported regions' phone numbers and email addresses are accepted. VoIP numbers and landlines are not supported.
</Callout>

## How do I edit my profile and avatar?
Navigate to:
**Kimi App** → **Profile** → **Settings** → **Personal Info** → Edit name, bio, or avatar → **Save**

<Callout type="warning">
**Note**: Your avatar and display name can be changed **up to 10 times within a 30-day period**. Please plan your changes accordingly.
</Callout>
