# Business cooperation & sales

<Callout type="info">
Partnership options for enterprise clients — from volume discounts to custom SLAs.
</Callout>

Kimi API offers a range of partnership options for enterprise clients to meet business needs at any scale.
## How to contact the sales team？
Visit [platform.moonshot.ai/contact-sales](https://platform.moonshot.ai/contact-sales) to submit a contact form. Our sales team will reach out within 1–2 business days.

<Callout type="tip">
**Include in your submission**:
- Company name and industry
- Estimated usage volume
- Primary use cases
- Preferred partnership model
</Callout>

## Contract signing
Organization-verified users can enter into a formal service agreement with Moonshot:
- Both standard service agreements and custom contracts are available.
- After signing, you gain access to wire transfer payments and enterprise-grade SLAs.
- Contact the sales team to discuss the specific process.

## Volume discounts
High-volume enterprise clients can apply for exclusive discounts:
| Requirement | Details |
| --- | --- |
| **Eligibility** | Organization verification + signed contract |
| **Discount levels** | Based on committed usage volume and contract duration |
| **Application** | Apply through sales team with estimated monthly usage |

## Enterprise API service
Enterprise API service builds on the standard API offering with:
- **Higher rate limits and concurrency quotas**
- **Dedicated technical support channel**
- **Custom SLA guarantees**
- **Early access to new features**
